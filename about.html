Are these products original? +

Can I see the import documents? +
Yes. Message us on WhatsApp and we send photos of the original purchase receipt and Pakistani customs clearance for your specific batch. This is a standing offer to every customer.

Contact us within 24 hours of delivery with a photo. We replace or fully refund — no arguments, no delays. We stand behind every order we send.

We ship across Pakistan. Lahore takes 1–2 working days. All other major cities take 2–3 working days. Delivery fee is PKR 200, free on orders over PKR 3,000.

Because this is the genuine imported product, legally cleared through Pakistani customs, with documented proof of origin. Cheaper local options are almost always counterfeit, expired, or grey-market goods with no accountability. You are paying for authenticity, documentation, and honesty.
