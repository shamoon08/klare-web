Our Import Journey

Every order follows the same documented, transparent process

1

🛍 Shopping in Germany

We make personal visits to DM stores across Germany, selecting products batch by batch and checking expiry dates and packaging integrity on the spot.

2

🧾 Store Receipts

Every purchase is receipted at the DM till. Original receipts are kept on file and available to share with any customer on WhatsApp request.

3

📦 Export Documentation

Products are professionally packed for international shipping. All items are listed accurately in export documentation — no misdeclaration, no shortcuts.

4

✅ Customs Clearance

Every shipment is officially declared and cleared through Pakistani customs with duty paid. You receive a legally imported product, not a smuggled one.

5

🔍 Quality Check

Before dispatch, every item is inspected for seal integrity, expiry date, and packaging condition. Anything below standard is set aside.

6

🚚 Cash on Delivery

Your order arrives at your door. You inspect it. You pay. No advance payment, no financial risk on your side — ever.

Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to the questions we get most

Are these products original? +
Yes. We buy them ourselves at DM Germany. We can show you the receipts — just ask on WhatsApp and we'll send photos within minutes.
Can I see the import documents? +
Yes. Message us on WhatsApp and we send photos of the original DM receipt and Pakistani customs clearance for your specific batch. This is a standing offer to every customer.
What if my product arrives damaged? +
Contact us within 24 hours of delivery with a photo. We replace or fully refund — no arguments, no delays. We stand behind every order we send.
Do you deliver to my city? +
We ship across Pakistan. Lahore takes 1–2 working days. All other major cities take 2–3 working days. Delivery fee is PKR 200, free on orders over PKR 3,000.
Why is the price higher than local options? +
Because this is the genuine imported product, legally cleared through Pakistani customs, with documented proof of origin. Cheaper local options are almost always counterfeit, expired, or grey-market goods with no accountability. You are paying for authenticity, documentation, and honesty.
